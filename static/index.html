Arcane Ledger

Welcome to Arcane Ledger

Catalog the collection. Build the decks. Find the cards.

Arcane Ledger is a Magic: The Gathering collection tracker powered by Scryfall catalog data and collector-owned pricing history.

What you can do

Built for collectors who actually play with their cards.

Catalog every card, every set Track your collection across Magic sets and exact card printings with Scryfall-backed detail.
Organize owned and wanted cards Keep tabs on what you own, what you are hunting, and what belongs on a wishlist.
Build and share virtual decks Design game-ready deck lists, even before every card is in your binder, then share them with friends.
Know where everything lives Map cards into binders, boxes, and other containers so storage stops being a guessing game.
Share wishlists and market extras Publish wishlists, list extra cards for sale, and give other collectors a clean way to browse.
Show off the stories Write quick blurbs about favorite pulls, leave public card comments, and keep private notes for yourself.
Track portfolio value See what you paid, what your cards are worth now, and how your collection changes over time.

CSV files can come from anywhere as long as the first row contains headers. Moxfield and Magic: The Gathering Arena CSV imports use Count, Name, Edition, Condition, Language, Foil, Collector Number, Alter, Playtest Card, and Purchase Price.
